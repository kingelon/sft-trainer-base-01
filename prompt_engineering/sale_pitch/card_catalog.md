# AMEX Card Product Catalog

## Business Cards
> For individual business owners / small businesses. Personal liability on the owner.

### Business Platinum Card
- **Annual Fee**: $895
- **Ideal For**: Business owners and frequent travelers
- **Key Benefits**: Premium travel experience, global lounge access, airline credits
- **Primary Focus**: Premium business travel and high-value spend
- **Best Signal Match**: High travel intensity, high total spend, hotel loyalty

### Business Gold Card
- **Annual Fee**: $375
- **Ideal For**: Businesses with high category-based spending
- **Key Benefits**: 4x points on top 2 eligible business spend categories each month (auto-selected from 6 categories)
- **Eligible 4x Categories**:
  - Media providers / advertising (online, TV, radio)
  - Electronic goods retailers, software & cloud system providers
  - Restaurants (including takeout and delivery)
  - Gas stations
  - Transit (trains, taxicabs, rideshare, ferries, tolls, parking, buses, subways)
  - Wireless telephone service (U.S. providers)
- **Additional**: 3x on flights and prepaid hotels booked on AmexTravel.com / Amex Travel App
- **Base**: 1x on all other eligible purchases
- **Primary Focus**: Maximizing business category spend
- **Best Signal Match**: High category-concentrated spend in ads, vendors, supplies

### Business Green Card
- **Annual Fee**: $95
- **Ideal For**: Everyday business purchases and occasional travel
- **Key Benefits**: Membership Rewards points on general business expenses
- **Primary Focus**: Simple business expense management
- **Best Signal Match**: General spend, low complexity, small teams, routine operational expenses

## Corporate Cards
> For companies with multiple employees. Company liability, central billing, administrator controls.

### Corporate Platinum Card
- **Annual Fee**: $550
- **Ideal For**: Senior executives and frequent travelers
- **Key Benefits**: Premium travel experience, easy administrator access, 5% cashbacks for employee cards, free airline credits, global lounge access
- **Primary Focus**: Travel
- **Best Signal Match**: Frequent exec travel, high spend, large company, company-level travel programs

### Corporate Gold Card
- **Annual Fee**: $250
- **Ideal For**: Travel and entertainment
- **Key Benefits**: Entertainment focused
- **Primary Focus**: Travel & entertainment
- **Best Signal Match**: Moderate travel, entertainment spend

### Corporate Green Card
- **Annual Fee**: $75
- **Ideal For**: Core employees doing day-to-day spending
- **Key Benefits**: Purchases, expenses, occasional travel
- **Primary Focus**: General purchases
- **Best Signal Match**: Basic travel program, routine employee expenses

### Corporate Meeting Card
- **Annual Fee**: $75
- **Ideal For**: Meetings and event transactions
- **Key Benefits**: Focused on meeting and event expenses
- **Primary Focus**: Meeting expenses
- **Best Signal Match**: Companies with heavy meeting/event spend

### Corporate Purchase Card
- **Annual Fee**: $0
- **Ideal For**: Purchasing business goods and services
- **Key Benefits**: Convenient fee-free option for procurement
- **Primary Focus**: Employee procurement card
- **Best Signal Match**: Procurement-heavy operations, need for employee purchasing controls

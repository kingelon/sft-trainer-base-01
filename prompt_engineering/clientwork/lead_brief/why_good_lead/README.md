# Why Good Lead Component

> **Status:** 🔲 Not started

Generates a reasoning-backed explanation of why a lead is a good prospect.

## Planned Output

A brief justification covering:
- Revenue and size signals
- Existing AMEX relationship (or lack of — acquisition opportunity)
- Spend patterns and category alignment
- Propensity model signals
- Competitive displacement opportunity

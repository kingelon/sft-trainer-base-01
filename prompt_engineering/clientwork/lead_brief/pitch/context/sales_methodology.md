## Sales Methodology

### Questioning Funnel
1. **Broad Open-Ended** → Industry, business health, plans for the year, rewards interest, top expenses
2. **Clarifying** → Drill into specifics from open-ended answers
3. **Close-Ended** → Confirm fit, get commitments

### Needs Identification
| Area | Discovers |
|---|---|
| Industry | # employees, expense categories, travel, vendors |
| Business priorities | Growth goals, reduce expenses, efficiency needs |
| Major expenses | Reward-eligible categories, current payment method |

### Payment Method Discovery
| Question | Purpose |
|---|---|
| How much by checks/debit/ACH? | Quantify switching benefit |
| Who else spends for the company? | Employee card opportunity |
| What other credit cards? | Competitive displacement |

## Qualifying Gate
- Annual revenue ≥ $4M (mandatory for corporate card programs)
- Below $4M may still qualify for Business cards

## Key Terminology
- **SME**: Small & Medium Enterprise
- **MR Points**: Membership Rewards points
- **Program Type**: Business (owner liability) vs Corporate (company liability)
- **Card Gap**: Whether prospect has no AMEX (New), lower-tier AMEX (Upgrade), or other AMEX products (Cross-sell)
